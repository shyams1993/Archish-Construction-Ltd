Design & Build

The traditional method for construction projects consists of the selection of a designer on one side, and the appointment of a contractor on the other side. Which a single point of responsibility is established by the client onto the general contractor. This in turns reduces risks and overall costs to the project.
Archish has excelled as a Design & Build contractor on various projects – as we have a network of highly skilled architects, engineers and workforce who we work with.
